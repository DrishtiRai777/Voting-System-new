Consent and Agreement

Organizational users registering with the College Voting System must acknowledge and adhere to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. This entails understanding and abiding by the regulations outlined in these documents. Please review and agree to proceed.

Consent for Data Collection

By registering with the College Voting System, organizations agree to the collection and processing of personal data for voting purposes, including organization name, contact details, and voting preferences.

Appointed Auditors

Appointed auditors will ensure fairness, transparency, and integrity in the voting process. Their role is vital for maintaining credibility and impartiality. Rest assured, they will diligently uphold these standards to preserve the integrity of the College Voting System.

Code of Conduct

Organizations registering must adhere to a code of conduct, committing to fair, transparent, and respectful behavior. This prohibits voter manipulation, coercion, or intimidation.
